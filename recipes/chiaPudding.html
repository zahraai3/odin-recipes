Chia Pudding

Description

Chia pudding is a nutritious and energizing choice packed with fiber, omega-3 fatty acids, and antioxidants. Just one serving can offer around 4–6 grams of protein, making it perfect for a light yet satisfying meal

Ingredients

  • Milk: Any type works—almond milk is light yet creamy.
  • Chia Seeds: Rich in omega-3s and protein; thicken the pudding naturally.
  • Sweetener: Maple syrup, honey, or agave for natural sweetness.
  • Vanilla: Optional, adds a rich, shop-like flavor.
  • Toppings: Fresh fruit, granola, yogurt, or nut butter—get creative!

Steps

  1. Pour milk into a medium bowl.
  2. Sprinkle chia seeds on top.
  3. Add maple syrup, vanilla, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Whisk immediately and vigorously to prevent clumping.
  5. Let sit for 10 minutes, then whisk again.
  6. Cover and refrigerate overnight until thick and creamy.
