Protein Brownies

These secretly healthy protein brownies are rich, chewy, fudgy, and absolutely melt-in-your-mouth amazing, with up to fifteen grams of protein each!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/3 cup protein powder
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup melted coconut oil or butter
  • 2 eggs or flax eggs
  • 2/3 cup sugar or erythritol
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease an 8-inch pan or line it with parchment paper.
  3. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients until combined.
  4. Pour batter into the pan and spread evenly.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes, then let cool completely.
  6. For best results, cover loosely and refrigerate overnight.
